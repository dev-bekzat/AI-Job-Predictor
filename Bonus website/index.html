🔮 Skill-Based Job Role Predictor

An AI-powered tool that analyzes your skills and predicts the most suitable job role for you.

Learn More

📘 Project Overview

This web application was developed as a final project for an Artificial Intelligence course. It shows how machine learning can help users understand their potential career path based on their skills.

Simply enter your skills (e.g., "Python, SQL, Excel"), and the AI will predict a suitable job role.

🧪 Dataset & Model

  • Dataset: Kaggle (≈300K records) + 10 manually added entries
  • Features: TF-IDF Vectorizer (top 1000 words)
  • Model: Random Forest Classifier (100 trees)
  • Split: 80% training / 20% testing
  • Tools: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Flask
